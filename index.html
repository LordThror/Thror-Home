The Bois

These are some underrated people you should check out on Scratch. Definitly check out Thintin and EpicBro90.

-Zoinks-

-Zoinks- is an amazing vector artist and game programmer. He placed in Vincrafts Game Jam, and also happens to be an even more incredible animator then he is game programmer. He's working on an RPG that sounds very promising.

Thintin

Thintin is an absolutly incredible RPG maker and pixel artist! Seriously, without hesitating I can say he is the most underrated person on Scratch. If you check any of these people out here check him and play Moonfall Mage and Raven Tale.

EpicBro90

EpicBro90 is probably the second most underrated person here. He is also probably the best vector artist on Scratch. His graphics and art blow my mind, and his programming is pretty incredible too. Defnitly go to his profile and click the blue button.

mister_dazzling

mister_dazzling is a very talented animator and creative story teller. His talents shine in the Exterra War and Scratch Court Case, and he doesn't limit his creativity to animations. He also has programmed a few RPGs, including Phantasie World.

RendHeaven

RendHeaven is a Scratch veteran of 10 years, and his ultimate talents are in his game design and cloud programming. Most of his projects have hit top-loved, though he has only shared four. He values strategic gameplay and his games have spawned an entire community on Scratch.

DawsonStudios

DawsonStudios is a cloud programmer, unfortunatly much underrated, and has worked on many collaboration projects. He loves fishing and hunting and many of his projects relect this. He has recently released a long time project, Battlefront.io WORLD and is currently working on collaborative projects.
