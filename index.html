News

Dreamtale: Order Reflections

Dreamtale: Order is the final edition of Dreamlands, besides probably some updates. I'd like to add some more sound effects and of course fix any bugs that players might encounter. I've dedicated a lot of my time to this project, and though it may not be perfect, I feel that I've accomplished something. When I started out making Dreamlands with mental images of Dreams and Nightmares, I thouhgt I was just going to make a quick, simple, casual RPG. It didn't turn out that way. What started as a quick maybe a month long at most project turned into a multiple month project. Some of this is due to a draining of my motivation at times and some real life complications like school and growing up, but I kept at it. And now I've finished it.

I'm not sure what projects I'll make after this. I really like the whole "world of dreams" concept I've come up with, and I'd like to expand that world. Perhaps make a completly open world project based on it, but that's really all dreaming. For now, I think I'll sit back and make some updates to The Dead of Winter, which has really the same background story as Dreamtale: Order. I've gotten mostly positive feedback on it, except that it's too easy and repetive, which I aim to fix with additional monsters, a boss, and some secrets...

My Favorite Pixel Art

The Boys

These are some underrated people you should check out on Scratch. Definitly check out Thintin and EpicBro90.

-Zoinks-

-Zoinks- is an amazing vector artist and game programmer. He placed in Vincrafts Game Jam, and also happens to be an even more incredible animator then he is game programmer. He's working on an RPG that sounds very promising.

Thintin

Thintin is an absolutly incredible RPG maker and pixel artist! Seriously, without hesitating I can say he is the most underrated person on Scratch. If you are going to check any of these people, check out him, and play Moonfall Mage and Raven Tale.

EpicBro90

EpicBro90 is probably the second most underrated person here. He is also probably the best vector artist on Scratch. His graphics and art blow my mind, and his programming is pretty incredible too. Definitly go to his profile and click the blue button.

mister_dazzling

mister_dazzling is a very talented animator and creative story teller. His talents shine in the Exterra War and Scratch Court Case, and he doesn't limit his creativity to animations. He also has programmed a few RPGs, including Phantasie World.

RendHeaven

RendHeaven is a Scratch veteran of 10 years, and his ultimate talents are in his game design and cloud programming. Most of his projects have hit top-loved, though he has only shared four. He values strategic gameplay and his games have spawned an entire community on Scratch. He has since left, but his games are still masterpieces.

DawsonStudios

DawsonStudios is a cloud programmer, unfortunatly much underrated, and has worked on many collaboration projects. He loves fishing and hunting and many of his projects relect this. He has recently released a long time project, Battlefront.io WORLD and is currently working on collaborative projects.

smorrebrot

Smorrebrot is an amazing programmer and vector artist. Most of his games are hack n' slash battlers, pitting yourself against waves of monsters. His crown jewel is probably his Tadek series (Tadek, Tadek 2, Tadek Tactics etc.).
